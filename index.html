Introducing Courtallam Cottage

Surround Yourself With Majestic Peaks & Aromatic Spice Breezes

Nestled among natural fruit plantations in Courtallam (Kuttralam), our private boutique eco-homestay delivers premium solace. Built with local materials (teakwood, high-grade basalt stone, and traditional terracotta floor tiling), each cottage honors organic estate layouts with contemporary resort interiors.

"Wake up to cardamom fragrances, spot endemic forest birds, take private river walks, and indulge in delicious South Indian meals crafted on authentic banana leaf spreads."
