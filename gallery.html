The Enchanting Healing Winds of Courtallam

Known across South Asian legend as the "Natural Spa of India," Courtallam is where nine major waterfalls dive from the sky. The air here holds therapeutic benefits because the waters rinse over three thousand endangered botanical medicinal herbs before arriving on the rocks.

Guests staying at Courtallam Cottage enjoy unique proximity keys to the Five Falls, Shenbagadevi, and tiger pools. Trek early in the morning through mist clouds to witness South India's premium monsoon sanctuaries in their rawest, purest splendor.
